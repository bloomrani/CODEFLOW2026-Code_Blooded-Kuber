The Mission

Simplify personal
finance with intelligence

This Application aims to solve that problem by creating an AI-powered application that can intelligently read and analyze a single Indian bank statement. The system automatically identifies transactions, organizes expenses and income into useful categories through managing statements, and converts complex financial data into simple, easy-to-understand insights. By giving users a clearer picture of their spending habits and financial patterns, the application helps them manage money more confidently and make better financial decisions in everyday life.

The objective is to encourage users to leverage Artificial Intelligence and data processing techniques to simplify personal finance analysis and improve financial awareness for millions of Indians.
